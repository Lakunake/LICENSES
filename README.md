## Quick Summary

**JACX-ANCSA-R v2.0** (Jan 2025): Foundation license for creative works + software. Strict NonCommercial interpretation. No monetization except minimal distribution costs. 17 sections, ~13.5k words.

**JACX-ANCSA-R v2.1** (Nov 2025): Enhanced version with AI training protections, trademark provisions, contributor governance, and compliance frameworks. Strict NonCommercial. 25 sections, ~19k words.

**JACX-GMG v1.0** (Nov 2025): Gaming-specific license enabling content creator monetization, mod donations, tournaments with prizes, and fan commerce while reserving commercial game distribution rights. Permissive NonCommercial interpretation for community activities. 27 sections, ~21k words.

**Core Difference:** ANCSA-R = "no money related to the work." GMG = "no commercial distribution of the game, but monetize your creative work about it freely.

## Use Case Recommendations

### Choose ANCSA-R v2.0 if:
- Software library, tool, or general creative work
- Want maximum control over all monetization
- Simple framework sufficient
- Not concerned about AI training
- Released before AI concerns emerged

### Choose ANCSA-R v2.1 if:
- Software library, tool, or general creative work
- Want AI training protection
- Need trademark provisions
- Multi-contributor project
- Export control or data protection requirements
- Want modern legal hardening

### Choose JACX-GMG v1.0 if:
- **Video game or interactive entertainment**
- Want content creator promotion (free marketing)
- Want thriving modding community
- Want competitive tournament scene
- Will rely on organic community growth
- Want to reserve only distribution rights
- Community monetization is strategic benefit

## What Each License PROHIBITS

### All Three Prohibit:
- Commercial distribution without license
- Removing attribution
- Violating ShareAlike (for derivatives)

### ANCSA-R v2.0 & v2.1 ALSO Prohibit:
- Any monetization beyond minimal distribution costs
- Platform-based revenue (YouTube ads, etc.)
- Donations beyond distribution costs
- Advertising-supported distribution

### JACX-GMG v1.0 ALSO Prohibits:
- Paywalling mods or requiring payment for access
- Commercial game distribution without license
- Selling game access or standalone modified games
- Gambling/wagering services
- AI training (except academic with conditions)
